What is Whole Life Insurance?


Whole Life Insurance is a type of permanent life insurance that provides coverage for your entire lifetime, as long as premiums are paid. Unlike term life insurance, which expires after a set period, whole life insurance combines a death benefit with a cash value component that grows over time. The premiums are typically fixed and guaranteed not to increase, and the policy builds cash value that you can access during your lifetime through loans or withdrawals.



Key Benefits of Whole Life Insurance

  1. Permanent coverage that lasts your entire lifetime
  2. Guaranteed premiums that never increase
  3. Cash value accumulation with tax-deferred growth
  4. Access to cash value through loans or withdrawals
  5. Guaranteed death benefit for your beneficiaries
