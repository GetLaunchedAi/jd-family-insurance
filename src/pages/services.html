Our Services

Final Expense Insurance

Designed to cover the costs associated with a person's funeral, burial, and other end-of-life expenses. A Final Expense policy can be a good choice for older individuals who want to lessen the financial burden on their loved ones after their passing. This specialized type of life insurance offers a lower coverage amount than traditional life insurance, and age limits are often higher â€” up to 85 with some policies. Qualifying can be simpler with a few health questions and little to no medical underwriting. Learn more about our Final Expense Insurance process.

Simplified Term Insurance

With simplified term life insurance, you'll go through a quicker qualification process. This kind of insurance may be ideal for those who do not want to wait for medical underwriting, or who want coverage to start as soon as possible. You will typically answer some health questions and may be able to get your approval instantly. Simplified term life insurance often has lower maximum age limits and a smaller face value, compared to term life with medical underwriting, but it still may be a great choice for your unique situation.

Mortgage Protection Insurance

Mortgage Protection Insurance is designed to protect your family from losing their home by covering mortgage payments during difficult times. This specialized insurance ensures your family can keep their home in the event of death, disability, or job loss. It can be structured to match your mortgage balance and provides peace of mind for homeowners. Learn more about our Mortgage Protection Insurance.

Whole Life Insurance

Whole Life Insurance provides permanent coverage that lasts your entire lifetime, combining a death benefit with cash value accumulation. Unlike term insurance, whole life offers guaranteed premiums that never increase and builds cash value that you can access during your lifetime. This type of insurance is ideal for those seeking lifelong protection with investment benefits. Learn more about our Whole Life Insurance.
